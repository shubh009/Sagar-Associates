Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a 3BHK interior design cost? +

Cost varies based on materials, scope, and finishes. We provide a tailored, transparent BOQ after our initial consultation.

How long does a complete home interior project take? +

A standard 3BHK takes 45-60 days from finalization of 3D designs to handover, tracked via rigorous milestones.

Do you manage civil work and contractors? +

Yes, we provide end-to-end services. Our reliable local NCR vendor network handles all civil, electrical, and plumbing modifications.

How does the 3D visualization phase work? +

After layout approval, we create photorealistic 3D renders of your space so you can see exactly how it will look before execution begins.

Do you incorporate Vastu Shastra principles? +

Absolutely. We have extensive experience deeply integrating Vastu principles into layouts without compromising modern aesthetics.

Do you provide a warranty on materials? +

Yes, we provide standard industry warranties on all structural materials, paints, and custom woodwork installed by our team.

Is there a charge for the first consultation? +

No, the initial requirement-gathering consultation is completely free of charge.

Do you undertake commercial interior projects? +

Yes, we design retail shops, corporate offices, restaurants, and hospitality spaces across NCR.
